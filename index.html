About Capstone High

Capstone High is a premier CBSE-affiliated school committed to academic excellence, creativity, and character building. Our mission is to foster curiosity and compassion among young minds.

WHY CAPSTONE HIGH ?

Capstone High, a CBSE-affiliated school in Hoskote, Bangalore, offers quality education from Pre-K to Grade 12, blending academic excellence with holistic development. Since 2014, we have nurtured curiosity, conviction, and compassion while equipping students with 21st-century skills. Recognized with the British Council’s International Dimensions in Schools Award, we provide comprehensive streams in Science, Humanities, and Commerce.

Located just 30 minutes from Whitefield, our well-connected campus ensures accessibility for nearby residential areas. With a mission to foster lifelong learning and meaningful relationships, Capstone High empowers students to achieve their best and thrive in a multicultural environment.
